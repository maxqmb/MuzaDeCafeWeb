Our Story

Muza de Cafe, which opened its doors on October 9, 2022, is inspired by the Polish word for "muse," symbolizing the source of inspiration that drives us to create a unique and exceptional coffee experience.

Our founders, Engr. Ruben Arciga and Lapurisima Arciga, are not only passionate about coffee but also have a rich family legacy. Their daughter, a beauty queen, embodies the same spirit of excellence and dedication that we strive to bring to every cup of coffee we serve.
